Four directions — one brand

Paper & Petals, four ways.

Four different feels for the same site. Same sections, same content — but each one carries a different mood. Scroll through all four, then tell me which one feels most like you. We can also mix elements from different directions if something catches your eye.

Heads up Photos are pulled straight from your current site, but you'll notice the same four images cycling through every section — your Showit site only exposes a handful publicly. They're also not necessarily in the right slots (a bouquet might show up where a tablescape would live). Focus on the overall feel, color, and pacing, not specific photo placement. Once you've picked a direction, we'll swap in the full-res originals and sort each one into the right spot.

Meet the Studio

An artist who happens to arrange flowers.

Paper & Petals was founded by TaLeah — an artist and floral designer bringing together two lifelong passions: a background in fine art and an eye for the composition of a room.

Every arrangement begins with the shape of the day itself — the venue, the light, the way people will move through the space. The florals are what tie it all together.

Founder & Designer TaLeah

What we do.

Three ways we work with couples and planners.

i.

Wedding Florals

Bouquets, ceremony pieces, reception tablescapes, and installations — designed as a coherent visual story from start to finish.

ii.

Private Events

Showers, engagements, anniversaries, and intimate dinners. Thoughtful florals for gatherings that don't need to feel like a production.

iii.

Custom Arrangements

A la carte pieces for milestones, gifts, photo shoots, and styled details. We'll work from your inspiration — not a preset menu.

the story

Two passions —
one little studio.

I started Paper & Petals because I couldn't pick between painting and arranging flowers — so I stopped trying. Every piece I design starts the way a painting does: with color, composition, and a little bit of feeling.

If you love flowers that feel lived-in, romantic, and just a little bit unexpected, we're probably going to get along great.

The Studio

Two passions, one studio.

Paper & Petals was founded by TaLeah — an artist and floral designer who believes that flowers are not décor, but the connective tissue of a celebration. Every bouquet, ceremony piece, and tablescape is arranged to feel both of the moment and out of time.

We work with couples who value craft. Who see the blooms as part of the story. Who want something that will still feel right ten years from now.

— TaLeah
