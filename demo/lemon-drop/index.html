Four directions — one brand

Lemon Drop, four ways.

Four different feels for the same site. Same sections, same inventory, same story — but each one carries a different mood. Scroll through all four, then tell me which one feels most like you. Mix-and-match also encouraged.

Heads up Your current site is on Wix, which lazy-loads all photos in the browser — so I could only scrape a handful of your actual images. The rest are stock placeholders from Unsplash for event rentals, weddings, and décor. Testimonials, contact info, and inventory categories are all real. Focus on feel, color, and pacing — we'll swap in your full photo library once you've picked a direction.

About

Born from a wedding of our own.

Lemon Drop started the week after our owner's 2023 wedding. She'd spent months chasing down details — the right chair, the right arch, the right candleholder — and realized most couples needed a single trusted source for all of it.

Since launching in 2024, we've helped with over 1,500 events across Utah. Every booking comes with a human on the other end of the email who has been exactly where you are.
