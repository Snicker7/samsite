§ 01 — The Studio

Flowers that hold the memory of the day.

Briar & Blossom Co. is the work of Maddy — a floral designer whose arrangements are less décor and more narrative. Each piece she designs starts with the couple, the venue, and the mood: the kind of floral story you'd find in a secret garden, straight out of a fairytale.

The studio specializes in lush, personal, statement-making florals — bouquets, ceremony pieces, reception tablescapes, installations. No preset menus. No filler. Just flowers that feel made for the one day they'll live in.

my story

Two florists in one —
artist & arranger.

I started Briar & Blossom because I couldn't stop thinking about flowers. I love the way a single bouquet can tie together a whole day — the dress, the venue, the light, the way people feel in the room.

I don't do preset packages or filler arrangements. Every piece I design is built around you — because your day deserves flowers that could only belong to you.
